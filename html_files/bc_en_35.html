FameLab is one of the biggest science communication competitions in the world.

If you think you can explain a scientific idea or research to a general audience, in just three minutes, then why not enter? You could become the new face of science, represent your country at the FameLab International final in the UK, and open doors to global opportunities in science communication!

Stay tuned for FameLab Thailand 2020!
