Learn English with the British Council and you’ll be learning with the world’s English experts.

We’ve been teaching English for more than 75 years and have helped more than 100 million people in 100 different countries improve their English skills and build their confidence.

You can learn English by taking face-to-face lessons in our teaching centres or at home via our online courses and learning tools. We can demonstrate your progress by providing you with internationally recognised proficiency tests.

Explore our unrivalled range of courses and support to meet your exact learning needs.

Teaching English. Supporting learning. Testing proficiency. 

In this section

English for young learners

Being confident in English can give your child an early advantage at school, helping them make the most of the education, qualifications and career opportunities that lie ahead.

English for adults

Being able to communicate in English can enhance your life in so many ways. It can improve your social life and career prospects, and can even help you make the most out of the internet.

English for IELTS preparation

As one of the co-creators of the IELTS exam, you can rest assured that the curriculum offered here meet the test requirements and enhance your skills necessary to get a better score.

Partner Schools and Universities

Our partnerships with Thai schools and universities provide support in English language learning to students of all ages, from kindergarten and primary to secondary and tertiary.

British Council's English language schools

At our conveniently located centres in Thailand, we offer you a full range of English courses, training programmes and workshops to support your English language learning.

Start learning with the world's English experts today

To register for a course you will first need to take a free level test so we can then help you choose the most suitable course for you.
