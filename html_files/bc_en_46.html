The United Kingdom offers you a world-leading education system, with UK qualifications that can make a real difference to your career.

Whether you want to come to the UK to study or find out more about UK higher education qualifications in your country, we are here to help you find your dream course and plan your studies.

Learn more

Why study in the UK?

From our world-recognised universities to our innovative approach to teaching and the leading minds who deliver it, we have what you need to reach your potential.

Scholarships

There are a number of scholarships to help you fund your studies, so make sure you don’t miss out on any financial help that may be available to you.

Living and studying in the UK

With diverse landscapes, a vibrant cultural offer and the chance to make friends from all over the world, time spent in the UK will be life-changing.

Education fairs and exhibitions

Come and meet the British Council and many of the UK's universities face-to-face.

Student stories

Read inspiring stories of the finalists of Alumni Awards 2017 and 2018

Find a UK Education Agent

There are education agents, counsellors and advisors who can help you to apply for a course at a university, college or school in the UK.

Subject highlights

No matter what you're passionate about, studying in the UK will help launch your career while offering amazing experiences.
