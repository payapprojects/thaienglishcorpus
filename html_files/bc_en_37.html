Every year, two million people take international exams with help from the British Council. Students and professionals gain qualifications that can open doors at leading academic institutions and improve their employment prospects around the world.

In this section

Take IELTS with the British Council

Work, learn and live in other countries by taking the IELTS test with the British Council. IELTS is the world’s most popular English language test. Book your test today.

IGCSE and AS/A Level

Looking for information on IGCSE and AS/A Level? School students, parents and private candidates can find out more about our school exams here.

Educational and professional exams

British Council is an authorised examinations centre for a wide range of educational and professional examinations on behalf of the UK's major examination boards.

Aptis - Assess English Skills

Choose Aptis to assess more people more efficiently and raise international English language standards in your organisation.

IELTS for schools and institutions

If you need to assess the language skills of your students, staff or potential candidates, IELTS is the only test you need.

Why take your exam with the British Council?

The rewards you will gain from your UK examination are worth it, whether it is to get a job, enter a university or start a new life in a different country.
