Benefits

  • Options to start planning for your future after retirement
    iRetire 1 : Single Premium Payment
    iRetire 5 : 5-year-Premium payment
  • Up to 20% annuity of Sum assured (iRetire5),
  • payable either monthly or annually to suit your needs
  • Life coverage of 200% of Sum assured or Cash Value, or 110% of total basic premium paid, whichever is higher (during annuity accumulation period)
  • Tax Deduction Benefits Up to 200,000 baht* (for annuity product)

* The basic premium of iRetire (excluding rider premiums) is applicable for individual income tax deduction up to 200,000 baht for annuity insurance, but not exceeding 15% of estimated income. The conditions are subject to The Revenue Department Announcement No.194 on Individual Income Tax as follows:
1. The annuity insurance policy is for a coverage period of 10 years and above
2. Regular annuity payouts
3. The annuity payouts are from 55 until 85 years of age, with completely fully paid premium artwork
