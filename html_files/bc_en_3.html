Through our qualifications, training courses, networking events and resources, we support teacher trainers and educators to develop their English teaching and language skills.

Our projects

Regional English Training Centres Project (RETC Project)

Regional English Training Centres Project (RETC Project) 

HSBC Kids Read

After 3 years of success in the Middle East and North Africa, HSBC Kids Read is now coming to Asia to support the development of reading both inside and outside of the classroom.

English for UN Peacekeepers

Since 2012, we have been working with the Thai Ministry of Foreign Affairs to develop English capacity to prepare soldiers and police officers for UN Peacekeeping Missions.

CEFR in Thailand

Find out how we have been training teachers in what CEFR standards in the Thai education system will mean for them and their students as well as the implications for classroom practice.

English for Education Professionals

Head teachers and education administrators are increasingly required to communicate in English for their work. Find out how the British Council is supporting them to develop their skills.

English We Can

The English We Can Project is a collaboration project with True Plookpanya to support primary sector English teachers to improve their own language skills and their classroom teaching.

Hornby Scholarships 2015

British Council Thailand and the A.S. Hornby Educational Trust are inviting applications from Thai English Language Teaching Professionals for 2015-16 Masters Degree scholarship programme.
