About

Located in Education Zone on the 7th floor of Central Plaza Chaengwattana, the centre is designed to provide our customers with better access to the all integrated services of the British Council in addition to our English language teaching operation.

The Chaengwattana centre offers a full range of English courses for all ages, either for general or academic purposes. In addition, IELTS Preparation courses are also offered for those who need to improve their performance in this important examination.

Every classroom in this new centre contains an interactive whiteboard and state-of-art modern teaching equipment linked to a computer, which helps students to learn English in a fun, friendly and effective way. This maintains the British Council  at the cutting edge of educational technology and ensures all students will have the best choice for English.
