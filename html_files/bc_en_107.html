What is myClub?

myClub is for all Adult students at the British Council. It gives you the opportunity to practise the English you’ve learned in class and learn about different aspects of culture from the UK and around the world. 

Offering a fun and relaxed way to use English, myClub is not a taught lesson. Each myClub has a different theme such as music, film or what’s going on in the news this week. It’s not just about English, it’s also about meeting people, making new friends and seeing a different side to our teachers. To make things more social, you can even bring a friend or family member.

To find out more about what’s happening in myClub this week, ask our Customer Services team for more information. You can sign up for myClub at reception or call 02 657 5678, 053 242 103 to reserve your place. See you at myClub!  if you would like to join any of the sessions. We look forward to seeing you there!

TED Talks

TED Talks are short, inspirational videos. In these sessions, we watch a video and talk about it. You can see the videos at www.ted.com. Come and be inspired!

Drama

Slang Sessions
