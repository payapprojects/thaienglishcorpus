Our Partners

Cheltenham Festivals

Cheltenham Festivals is the charitable organisation behind the town’s internationally acclaimed Jazz, Science, Music and Literature Festivals. Through cutting edge and creative programming, Cheltenham’s four inspirational festivals have been at the fore-front of the UK’s cultural scene since the inaugural Music Festival in 1945.

  • http://www.cheltenhamfestivals.com/

Ministry of Science and Technology

The Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST) is the principle agency in developing science, technology and innovation for the purposes of solving the country's socio-economics problems and enhancing its long-term competitive capacity.

  • http://www.most.go.th/main/

NSTDA

National Science and Technology Development Agency (NSTDA) is a home of four national research centers: BIOTEC, MTEC, NECTEC, NANOTEC and one technology management center: TMC

  • http://www.nstda.or.th/

STI

National Science Technology and Innovation Policy Office principally works with the industries, government, academia and local communities. Collaborative networking is an essential part of the office’s strategy and is emphasized by the creation and promotion of active collaboration through strong linkages and exchange programs with local, overseas, and international organizations.

  • http://www.sti.or.th

True Together

True Corporation Plc is Thailand’s only fully integrated communications provider and leader in convergence, offering an unrivalled selection of products and services covering mobile, broadband, pay TV, and content.

  • http://www3.truecorp.co.th/home

National Science Museum (NSM)

National Science Museum (NSM) and The Ministry of Science and Technology aim to build knowledges and understanding of science and technology for youth and public with exhibition and activities to promote learning experiences. The NSM’s first museum, The Science Museum was opened since 2000.

  • http://www.nsm.or.th/english/index.php

Chulalongkorn University

Located in the centre of Bangkok, Chulalongkorn University is the oldest and most prestigious institution of higher learning in Thailand. It is also one of the leading national research universities. With over 35,000 undergraduates and postgraduates, its mission is to be the center for academic learning and professional excellence, educating students with professional know-how and research skills, as well as preserving the arts, culture, and values.

  • https://www.chula.ac.th/en/

The Standard

Thailand’s news agency, THE STANDARD artistically presents ideas, thoughts, and knowledge that build understanding and passion in people. Its ultimate aim is to create a positive change to the society.

  • https://thestandard.co/

Euraxess ASEAN

EURAXESS ASEAN is a networking tool supporting ASEAN researchers to connect with European researchers. It also supports researchers in Europe looking to collaborate with partners in ASEAN. 

EURAXESS ASEAN provides information about research funding and mobility opportunities, European research policies as well as training and networking opportunities.  Membership in the network is free of charge. 

  • https://euraxess.ec.europa.eu/worldwide/asean
