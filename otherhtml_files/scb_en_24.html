Siam Commercial Bank PCL., Yangon Representative Office

One of the world’s current investment hotspots, with direct land link to China, India and Thailand, Myanmar is home to 54 million people and undergoing significant political and economic reforms. The country represents great potential for development and infrastructure related business opportunities. GDP growth going forward is expected to average 8% over next few years. SCB representative office in Yangon along with its local partner banks are ready to serve your financial needs.
