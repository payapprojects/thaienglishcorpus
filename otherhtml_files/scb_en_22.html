Turn the regular holiday to a memorable experience at Holiday Inn Resort Vana Nava Hua Hin

Bryde's whale 5.5 meters, is jumping from the water, inviting me to pick up the camera to take the picture immediately right after arrived at Holiday Inn Resort Vana Nava Hua Hin.

In the past Hua Hin was only a small fishing village but after the Southern Railway completed at the end of the reign of King Rama VI, it brought prosperity to Hua Hin. and change the small village to become a beautiful vacation place from that day until today. By combining the atmosphere of the sea, fish and fishing with the modern era, making it a special design that is different from other places.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

When entering the hotel, press the elevator to the 8th floor “Sky Lobby "with staff wearing a dress made from indigo dyed fabrics like bright sea water standing smiling, waiting to welcome guests. Upon check-in completed, we will get a wristband, used to access the elevator, open the room door and entered the Vana Nava Water Park. This wristband can also be used to pay for all food and service fees in the water park. Can choose either prepaid or will be included in the payment when checking out.

Look at the accommodation, there are 6 types of rooms here

 
 
 
 

Vana Nava Room

The 39 square meter room is decorated in a serene style, feeling like home with a balcony and chairs that guests can relax and enjoy the mountain view. Inside the room also has a comfortable sofa.

 
 
 
 

Ocean View Room

According to the name of this room Of course, we can see the sea view from the room. The room size and decoration are the same as the Vana Nava room, but when the eyes open in the morning, the image in the front is a clear blue sea instead.

 
 
 
 

Kids Suite Room

No room is suitable for families with children more than this room with 52 square meters space. There is enough space for children. And their bunk bed. There is also a bathtub to enjoy the warm bath. and a long sofa on the balcony for the family to have a great time with smiles when watching the sea view and mountains together.

 
 
 
 

Ocean Suite

This room is located at the corner and has an area of ​​68 sq. Guests can enjoy the sea view all day long, whether in the bedroom or living room that clearly separated and comes with 2 bathrooms. The special feature of this room is that it can be connected to the Ocean View room so that it has a combined area of ​​more than 100 square meters. It is suitable for families who come with older children or come with a group of close friends.

 
 
 
 

Panoramic Suite

Panoramic sea view in front of you in a 50 sq.m. room ready to welcome the dawn of the new day with the sunrise view. Waking you up with the soft sunlight, get up and enjoy the breakfast and go down to enjoy the water park all day. If accompanied by friends, can also connect to the Ocean View room as well.

 
 
 
 

Sky Suite

The widest room with an area of ​​128 sq.m., plus located on the top floor so the guests can see the lively life of Hua Hin and the sea view in the same time. This room also increases the proportion with the dining area and living room separated from the bedroom with sliding doors. Specialty of the dining table in the room Is that you can change the ordinary table to a pool table to create fun activity before going to bed.

 
 
 
 

The hotel restaurant is another factor in choosing accommodation. Let's see the restaurant here.” Plamong Restaurant” located on the 7th floor, so you can enjoy the food with a view of Hua Hin city.  Fresh variety of dishes line up to fill your stomach all day long because it is open for All Day Dining.

 
 
 
 

The hotel restaurant is another factor in choosing accommodation. Let's see the restaurant here.” Plamong Restaurant” located on the 7th floor, so you can enjoy the food with a view of Hua Hin city.  Fresh variety of dishes line up to fill your stomach all day long because it is open for All Day Dining.

 
 
 
 

It has been said that this is a family-friendly hotel, of course, there must be "Bruda Kids Club" which comes in a bright sea theme suitable for users. Have toys and player to enhance kids’ development and activities suitable for children of all ages.
If parents want to go to the spa, can take the kids and leave them here. Because there are nannies to look after the children.

 
 
 
 

There are many beautiful spots here. If you want swim or enjoy the pool but need a privacy recommended to come up to the 26th floor. Will find an Infinity Pool with panoramic sea views. Either relaxing the body and mind in the jacuzzi, swim gently or sunbathing are all great to do there. If you come with child, there is also a small slider that kid can play. If you want freshness, you can order smoothies, cocktails or simple dishes and eat by the pool as well. At night time, you may be able to imagine that at this height how stunning the scene will be, just enjoy the stars and sky up there.

From the pool If walking up the stairs to another floor, will find “Vana Nava Sky”. Sky view observation with glass platform that you will see below is Vana Nava Water Park. With clear glass floors extending from the roof of the hotel on a height of 110 m from the ground. Here is the roof Top bars and restaurants designed by world-renowned designer Ashley Sutton. And a fine dining menu from Joseph Boroski, the creator of world-class cocktail menus, creating original cocktails that match appetizing dishes from the hotel's chefs’ team.

For those who want to experience the sea. The hotel also has a shuttle bus to Le Colonial Beach Club, blue-and-white colonial-style vacation home with a beach bed to sleep and listen to the waves. Then order drinks from various menu from bar, whether fresh fruit juice or refreshing cocktails. Or to choose to taste food from a great chef are great experience.
